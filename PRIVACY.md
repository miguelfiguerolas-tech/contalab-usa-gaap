# Privacy Policy — ContaLab US GAAP

_Last updated: June 22, 2026_

ContaLab US GAAP ("the extension") is a local accounting tool for students. This
policy explains how it handles your data. In short: **it doesn't collect, transmit,
sell, or share any data. Everything you enter stays on your device.**

## What data the extension handles

All data you create — accounting periods, the chart of accounts, journal entries,
and the reports generated from them — is stored **locally in your browser** using
IndexedDB and `localStorage`. This data never leaves your device and is never sent
to us or to any third party.

## What the extension does NOT do

- It does not collect personal information.
- It does not transmit any data to remote servers.
- It does not use analytics, tracking, or cookies.
- It does not require an account or sign-in.
- It does not sell or share data with third parties.
- It works fully offline.

## Permissions

- **`storage` / `unlimitedStorage`** — used solely to save your accounting data
  locally in the browser so your work persists between sessions. No data is uploaded.

## Data you export

You may, at your own initiative, export your work to a JSON, PDF, or CSV file. Those
files are saved to your device by you; the extension does not transmit them anywhere.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this same URL with a
new "Last updated" date.
